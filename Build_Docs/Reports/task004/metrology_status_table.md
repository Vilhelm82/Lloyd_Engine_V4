# Metrology Status Table

| Status | Input condition | Value shape | Validity mapping | Conditioning behavior | Protocol behavior | Refusal or indeterminate behavior |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| `noise_floor_declared` | finite nonnegative declared floor | `NoiseFloorValue` with method `declared`, sample count 0, empty observations | defined, finite, selectable, not advanceable, observable | well-conditioned | produced by `B_K_NOISE_FLOOR_PROTOCOL` | invalid floor raises protocol error |
| `noise_floor_estimated` | non-empty finite observations, `max_abs_observed` | `NoiseFloorValue` with max absolute observation and sample evidence | defined, finite, selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | produced by `B_K_NOISE_FLOOR_PROTOCOL` | invalid method or observation raises protocol error |
| `noise_floor_indeterminate` | empty observations | `NoiseFloorValue` with `noise_floor=None` and empty observations | not defined, not finite, not selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | accepted by limit-of-detection classifier | downstream classification becomes `detection_indeterminate` |
| `detected` | `abs(observable) > L` | `LimitOfDetectionValue` with `above_limit` comparison | defined, finite, selectable, not advanceable, observable | well-conditioned | produced by limit-of-detection classification | none |
| `below_limit_of_detection` | `abs(observable) <= L` and `L > 0` | `LimitOfDetectionValue` with `below_limit` or `on_limit` comparison | defined, finite, not selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | produced by limit-of-detection classification | does not certify identity-zero |
| `detection_indeterminate` | indeterminate floor, or zero observable with zero floor and no identity evidence | `LimitOfDetectionValue` with indeterminate comparison | not defined, finite, not selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | produced by limit-of-detection classification | preserves floor trace and raw observable |
| `identity_zero` | exact zero observable with explicit identity evidence | `LimitOfDetectionValue` with `identity_zero_evidence` comparison | defined, finite, selectable, not advanceable, observable | well-conditioned | produced by limit-of-detection classification | nonzero observable with identity evidence raises protocol error |
| `calibration_valid` | finite nonzero K_q through ProjectiveRatio and scalarization | `ProxyCalibrationValue` with projective coordinates, projective trace, scalar trace, and scalar K_q | defined, finite, selectable, not advanceable, observable | well-conditioned | accepted by `VALID_PROXY_CALIBRATION_PROTOCOL` | pointwise only; no stability claim |
| `calibration_invalid` | zero proxy against nonzero transfer, or nonzero proxy against zero transfer | `ProxyCalibrationValue` preserving ProjectiveRatio status and evidence | defined, not finite, not selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | refused by valid-calibration consumer | no scalar infinity or numeric sentinel |
| `calibration_indeterminate` | zero proxy against zero transfer | `ProxyCalibrationValue` preserving ProjectiveRatio indeterminate status and refusal evidence | not defined, not finite, not selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | refused by valid-calibration consumer | no NaN or numeric sentinel |
| `proxy_uncalibrated` | missing calibration evidence | `ProxyCalibrationValue` with reason and no K_q evidence | not defined, not finite, not selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | refused by valid-calibration consumer | missing evidence is typed, not exceptional |
