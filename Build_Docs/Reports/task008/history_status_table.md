# History Status Table

| status | input condition | value shape | validity mapping | conditioning behavior | protocol behavior | refusal or incomplete behavior |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `history_event_recorded` | A V4 `TypedResult` is recorded with stream ID, observation key, and sequence index. | `StatusEventValue` | defined, finite, selectable, not advanceable, observable | well-conditioned | emitted by `HISTORY_EVENT_PROTOCOL` | no refusal |
| `history_transition_stable` | Adjacent comparable events preserve protocol, status family, status, validity, and geometry signature. | `StatusTransitionValue` | defined, finite, selectable, not advanceable, observable | well-conditioned | emitted by `HISTORY_TRANSITION_PROTOCOL` | no refusal |
| `history_transition_protocol_changed` | Adjacent comparable events have different source protocol identities. | `StatusTransitionValue` | defined, finite, selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | emitted by transition protocol | evidence status only; not a stable-trace refusal until consumed |
| `history_transition_status_family_changed` | Protocol matches but status enum family differs. | `StatusTransitionValue` | defined, finite, selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | emitted by transition protocol | evidence status only |
| `history_transition_status_changed` | Protocol and family match but status value differs. | `StatusTransitionValue` | defined, finite, selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | emitted by transition protocol | evidence status only |
| `history_transition_validity_changed` | Protocol, family, and status match but validity differs. | `StatusTransitionValue` | defined, finite, selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | emitted by transition protocol | evidence status only |
| `history_transition_geometry_changed` | Protocol, family, status, and validity match but supplied geometry signatures differ or one is absent. | `StatusTransitionValue` | defined, finite, selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | emitted by transition protocol | evidence status only |
| `history_transition_incomparable` | Events differ by stream/key, have non-increasing sequence index, or are not recorded event results. | `StatusTransitionValue` | not defined, not finite, not selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | emitted by transition protocol | trace construction treats this as incomplete |
| `history_trace_empty` | Trace requested with no events. | `StatusTraceValue` | not defined, not finite, not selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | emitted by `HISTORY_TRACE_PROTOCOL` | refused by stable-trace protocol |
| `history_trace_singleton` | Trace has exactly one recorded event. | `StatusTraceValue` | defined, finite, not selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | emitted by trace protocol | refused by stable-trace protocol |
| `history_trace_stable` | Trace has two or more ordered comparable events and all adjacent transitions are stable. | `StatusTraceValue` | defined, finite, selectable, not advanceable, observable | well-conditioned | accepted by `STABLE_HISTORY_TRACE_PROTOCOL` | no refusal |
| `history_trace_transitioned` | Trace has at least one non-stable comparable adjacent transition. | `StatusTraceValue` | defined, finite, selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | emitted by trace protocol | refused by stable-trace protocol |
| `history_trace_incomplete` | Inputs are not valid event results, stream/key evidence is mixed, or pairwise comparison is incomparable. | `StatusTraceValue` | not defined, not finite, not selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | emitted by trace protocol | refused by stable-trace protocol |
| `history_trace_unordered` | Events share stream/key but sequence indices are not strictly increasing. | `StatusTraceValue` | not defined, not finite, not selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | emitted by trace protocol | refused by stable-trace protocol |
