| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

| `solver_converged_identity` | Task 004 residual detection emits `identity_zero` with explicit identity evidence. | `SolverStepValue` or `SolverRunValue` | defined, finite, selectable, not advanceable, observable | well-conditioned | accepted by converged solver protocol | terminal convergence |
| `solver_converged_below_detection` | Residual is below detection and policy permits below-detection termination. | `SolverStepValue` or `SolverRunValue` | defined, finite, selectable, not advanceable, observable | well-conditioned | accepted by converged solver protocol | terminal convergence, not identity-zero |
| `solver_projection_blocked` | Projection has no advance-defining root or unique projection. | `SolverStepValue` or `SolverRunValue` | defined, not finite, not selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | refused by converged protocol | terminal block |
| `solver_tangent_blocked` | Projection is tangent contact and Task 003 marks advance invalid. | `SolverStepValue` or `SolverRunValue` | defined, finite, selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | refused by converged protocol | terminal block |
| `solver_selection_refused` | Requested branch is incompatible and projection selection refuses. | `SolverStepValue` or `SolverRunValue` | not defined, not finite, not selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | refused by converged protocol | terminal refusal |
| `solver_branch_unidentified` | Policy requires branch fingerprint evidence and supplied evidence is missing, incomplete, or unidentified. | `SolverStepValue` or `SolverRunValue` | defined, finite, not selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | refused by converged protocol | terminal branch block |
| `solver_proxy_uncalibrated` | Policy requires branch evidence and proxy or Kq evidence is uncalibrated. | `SolverStepValue` or `SolverRunValue` | not defined, not finite, not selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | refused by converged protocol | terminal proxy block |
| `solver_refinery_rejected` | Policy requires refinery acceptance and Task 007 accepted-rewrite protocol refuses. | `SolverStepValue` or `SolverRunValue` | defined, finite, not selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | refused by converged protocol | terminal refinery block |
| `solver_history_unstable` | Projection history is transitioned, incomplete, unordered, or refused while stable history is required. | `SolverRunValue` | defined, finite, not selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | refused by converged protocol | terminal history block |
| `solver_sequence_inconsistent` | Caller model sequence is unordered, repeated, or state continuity fails by exact Python equality. | `SolverRunValue` | not defined, not finite, not selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | refused by converged protocol | terminal sequence block |
| `solver_step_budget_exhausted` | Supplied finite model budget is consumed without convergence or terminal block. | `SolverRunValue` | defined, finite, not selectable, not advanceable, observable | well-conditioned | refused by converged protocol | terminal budget evidence |
| `solver_protocol_refused` | Required typed evidence cannot be consumed by its protocol. | `SolverStepValue` or `SolverRunValue` | not defined, not finite, not selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | refused by converged protocol | typed refusal evidence preserved |
| `solver_indeterminate` | Evidence is insufficient for a more specific solver status. | `SolverStepValue` or `SolverRunValue` | not defined, not finite, not selectable, not advanceable, observable | warning | refused by converged protocol | indeterminate terminal evidence |
